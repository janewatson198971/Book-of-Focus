What I’m building

A practical, lightweight focus companion. The goal is to help users stay consistent without noise, tracking, or hidden data flows.

  • Simple and clear UX
  • Fast performance
  • Privacy-by-design

Transparency

I want moderators and users to see a clean picture: who builds the app, how it works, and what it does with data. Book of Focus is designed to operate without collecting personal data.

  • No selling of data
  • No sharing to third parties
  • No hidden analytics

Monetization plan

Right now there are no paid features and no subscriptions. In the future I may add optional purchases/subscriptions to fund development.

  • Optional, transparent pricing
  • Clear value for paid features
  • Respectful free experience

Roadmap (high-level)

This is a direction, not a promise — priorities can change based on feedback and stability.

Now
Improve stability, refine UX, fix bugs quickly, and keep privacy simple and clear.
Next
Add new helpful features based on real user requests, while staying lightweight and fast.
Later
Introduce optional monetization (premium features) to support ongoing development and updates.
Always
Maintain transparency: clear policies, accessible support, and honest product communication.
